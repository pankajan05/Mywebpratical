About Meemure

Meemure is a village in SriLanka with a population of about 400. It is located near the border between Kandy District and Matale District in the Knukles Mountain Range Meemure is one of the most remote villages in Srilanka with the only acess via a 15 km (8.7k.m) trail from the town of Loolwatte. There is no cellular service availabel in the village. but a CDMA telephone service is availabel. There is no direct mail delivery to the village; a villager journeys each day to Thapal Junction to exchange incoming and outgoing postal mail with a postman.
